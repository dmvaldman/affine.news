What is this?

Affine.news helps you see bias in news reporting. Instead of reading the news about your own country, you see what other countries are saying. Some perspectives line up, some clash. You’ll discover stories you never even knew happened, simply because your country didn’t care enough to cover them.

How does it work?

We collect international coverage from major outlets around the world and translate them into English. Think of it as: “What is Country A saying about Country B?” Then we give you a search and browse interface to compare those voices side by side.

Why is this called "Affine News"?

In math, an affine space has no notion of a "center". You can't talk about the absolute position of something, only the relative position between two things. Similarly here, we are judging news by the relative position of one country to another.
